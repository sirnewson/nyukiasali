Roots in the Wild

"Nyuki Asali" means Bee Honey in Swahili. It represents a bridge between two worlds: the rolling plains of Oklahoma and the vibrant red soil of Kenya.

We don't just sell honey. We harvest hope. Every jar tells a story of survival, resilience, and the sweet promise of a new beginning for young adults aging out of the orphanage system.

The Impact Circle

We focus on the critical transition period when support systems usually disappear.

Transitional Housing
