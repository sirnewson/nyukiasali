Where Nature Meets Nurture

Nyuki Asali, "Bee Honey" in Swahili, was born from a deep love for Kenya's natural beauty and a commitment to its future generation. We believe in the power of community and the sweetness of pure, wild honey.

Our partnership with local beekeepers honors ancient traditions, delivering honey as vibrant as the land itself. Each purchase directly supports youth transitioning from orphanages to independence.

Common Questions

Is your honey truly raw and organic?

Absolutely. Our honey is harvested directly from wild hives in Kenya and is never heated, pasteurized, or processed. We add no sugars or additives, ensuring you get 100% pure, natural honey.

How do my purchases support Kenyan youth?

100% of our profits go directly to our mission. The funds are used to provide essential services like transitional housing, job skills training, mental health support, and case management for young adults aging out of the orphanage system in Kenya.

Where do you ship to?

Currently, we ship to all locations within Kenya and are working on expanding to international shipping soon. Sign up for our newsletter to be the first to know when we expand our shipping zones!
