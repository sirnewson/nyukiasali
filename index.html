Our Mission

All proceeds from Nyuki Asali go towards helping Kenyan orphans aging out of orphanages with transitional housing, case management, mental health assistance, and skills training.

Where Nature Meets Nurture

Nyuki Asali, "Bee Honey" in Swahili, was born from a deep love for our Kenyan heritage, right here in Oklahoma, USA. As Kenyans living abroad, we wanted to build a bridge back home, connecting the sweetness of pure, wild honey with a powerful mission.

Our apiary in Oklahoma is where our journey begins. Soon, we will expand our hives to the beautiful landscapes of Kenya, working hand-in-hand with local beekeepers and honoring the ancient traditions that make our honey as vibrant as the land itself.

Common Questions

Is your honey truly raw and organic?

Absolutely. Our honey is harvested directly from wild hives and is never heated, pasteurized, or processed. We add no sugars or additives, ensuring you get 100% pure, natural honey.

How do my purchases support Kenyan youth?

100% of our profits go directly to our mission. The funds are used to provide essential services like transitional housing, job skills training, mental health support, and case management for young adults aging out of the orphanage system in Kenya.

Where do you ship to?

Currently, we ship to all locations within the USA. We plan to expand to international shipping, including Kenya, very soon. Sign up for our newsletter to be the first to know!
